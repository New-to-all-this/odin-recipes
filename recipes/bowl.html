Bowl

Description

Looking for something quick and easy that is also delicious and healthy?

Then a bowl is the right choice for you. You can combine any vegetables that look good to you or just might be leftover in your fridge. With some carbohydrates and protein this will be filling and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • raw vegetables
  • tofu
  • some lettuce
  • dressing

Steps

  1. chop everything to the size you like
  2. fry tofu
  3. arrange everything in a bowl and add dressing
