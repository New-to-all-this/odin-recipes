Noodle salad

Description

Quick and easy this salad comes with lots of carbohydrates to make you happy.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • olive oil
  • small tomatoes
  • sun dried tomates
  • olives
  • rucola
  • pine nuts

Steps

  1. cook noodles and let them cool off a bit
  2. mix them with olive oil
  3. roast pine nuts gently and let them cool off, too
  4. add everything to the noodles and mix well
